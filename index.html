Queen Culture Grunge / Shoegaze

2014–2017

This was the band I toured heavily with, and it’s still some of my favorite music I’ve made. I played bass and guitar, and eventually took over vocals after our guitarist Todd started chain-smoking. We recorded the entire record live on analog (except “Blend In,” which was part of another EP). I even had to call our 14-year-old drummer out of school because we recorded during the week when studio time was cheaper.
